About Us

Headquartered in Shanghai, MeldaTech is at the forefront of AI services. Founded by Zakir Ullah and Oksana Tovmachenko in 2017, we empower businesses by delivering scalable, precise solutions tailored to their data needs.

Our Services

Data Crawling

We use advanced web crawlers to source and download data for our customers. Our expertise includes:

  • Dynamic web scraping for large-scale data extraction.
  • Automated data retrieval from APIs and JavaScript-rendered content.
  • Content monitoring for real-time updates.

Annotation

We label data using large language models (LLMs) and human supervision to ensure high accuracy. Examples include:

  • Text annotation for entity recognition and sentiment analysis.
  • Semantic segmentation and bounding box annotation for images.
  • Video annotation for motion tracking and event detection.

Labeling

We provide precise and scalable labeling services for diverse datasets:

  • Classification of text, images, and audio for machine learning.
  • Multi-label tagging and domain-specific categorization.

End-to-End Model Learning

Comprehensive solutions for machine learning workflows:

  • Preprocessing and cleaning raw datasets.
  • Training and fine-tuning deep learning models.
  • Deploying models using cloud-native architectures.
